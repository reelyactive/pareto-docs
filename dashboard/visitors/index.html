Visitors Dashboard Tab
Observe devices likely to carried by people, on the basis of sessions

The visitors perspective is the default for the dashboard. The visitors tab provides a graphical overview of occupancy: how many people are where?

Active Visitors

The Active Visitors banner displays all occupants with an active session. Each occupant is represented graphically as a bubble and the total occupant count is provided. It is possible to toggle between the compact and expanded view by clicking on Show all Visitors.

The View Report button is a shortcut to the Analytics View and the Manage button is a shortcut to the People and Things View .


Points of Interest

The Points of Interest banner displays all places having at least one active visitor. Each point of interest is represented graphically as a bubble and the total count is provided.

The View Status button is a shortcut to the Infrastructure Status and the Manage button is a shortcut to the Points of Interest View .


Device Segmentation

The Device Segmentation chart provides a breakdown of the types of devices by which the active visitors are detected.



POI Popularity

The Point of Interest Popularity chart provides a breakdown of occupancy by directory (place).
